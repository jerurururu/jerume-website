  • ECO-SPORK

    Our research team has developed a sustainable eco-spork prototype crafted from recycled paper reinforced with tree resin. This initial model is part of our study focused on creating an environmentally friendly, durable, reusable, and safe utensil for daily use. The current prototype represents an early phase of testing where we are actively experimenting with materials, molding techniques, and protective coatings to assess its strength and functionality. We are continuing our research to refine the product's shape, texture, and overall durability before finalizing the design.

  • EXPERIMENT

    The chosen experiment is the balloon and plastic experiment, where when the balloon is rubbed on your hair, the triboelectric effect happens. The balloon gains electrons and becomes negatively charged, while your hair loses electrons and becomes positively charged. What will happen there is that the balloon will float up and will not fall down due to the plastic supporting the balloon.
